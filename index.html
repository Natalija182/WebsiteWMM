Bio

Throughout the 25 years I've been in the Music Business I have had various roles:

Scheer

Professional Musician / Manager

Jan 93 - Jan 99

I started my music career in this professional band and in the early days handled all management requirements. I was mainly the bass player but also on studio recordings played acoustic and keyboards. We were signed to Mother Records Dublin (U2 label) and then in 1995 we signed a worldwide deal to 4AD Records for UK/Europe and Warner Bros for US/Asia. We released 2 albums with total sales of 250,000units. We released a number of EPs and singles and toured the UK/Europe/US and Asia extensively for 3 years. I learnt a lot about the music business and when we split up in 2000 I took this knowledge and fourged a career in the music business.

Schism PR/Records

Owner

June 03 – Jan 09

I have a wealth of experience in marketing & promotion looking after PR campaigns for such international artists as Dolly Parton, The Strokes, Brian Wilson (Beach Boys), Tom Waits etc. I planned and implemented all aspects of their campaign covering TV, Press & Radio, Digital including interviews & concerts.

Riverman Records Ltd

Label Manager

Feb 99 – Sept 03

Riverman Records was a major independent label based in London with an annual budget of 2 million pounds and investment partners such as Ministry Of Sound & Sanctuary Records. As Label Manager I over seen all departments including A&R, Marketing & Finance and work closely with the Board of Directors implementing their vision of the company. I was involved in signing artists, taking them through the recording process and launching/releasing their albums and working on their marketing campaigns.

The Weatherbies Ltd

Business Development Consultant & Creative Content Developer

Jan 10 – Present

The Weatherbies is a Children's Animation project. As Business Consultant I have steered the company through initial Proof of Concept, Market Research, developing & designing characters, writing scripts & creating industry bible to developing a 5-minute animation pilot ready for pitching to UK & International Broadcasters.

White Mountain Music Ltd.

Music Publisher/Composer

Jan 09 – Present

I have been involved with Publishing for 11 years now. I have arranged, recorded, produced and collaborated with all my artists over the years. I was forced to take a hiatus from the music business when I took a stroke in the summer of 2017. I was not as active in the music business while I was recouping from my illness but continued to song write as my love of music and the joy it brought me and its healing power with regards the stroke was vital in my recovery. In 2020 I started to compose music for moving image. As a Publisher I successfully placed other artists music on major US dramas such as 'Keeping Up With The Kardashians & Hart Of Dixie. I would also set up co-writing opportunities in Nashville, New York, LA & London. Now I am concretating on composing, songwriting and collabroating on projects.
